    • Critical employee left your firm?
    Learn how Mutual Force can help you.

    When a critical employee leaves a firm, it usually takes time and money for an employer to effectively find a replacement or educate current employees to substitute the gap. The replacement or current employee(s) may struggle to replace the position due to lack of specific knowledge the ex-employee already has. We, at Mutual force using our patent pending process help employers gain the critical knowledge of your process or system an ex-employee holds when he or she decides to leave. We do this by providing a communication medium where employers after the employee leaves the firm can confidentially seek the expert knowledge the ex-employee has. The ex-employee can respond to the employer queries confidentially as per a contract from anywhere and anytime using mutualforce.com.

    Because you maintain a contact with your ex-employees after they leave your company, Mutual Force provides an added advantage if you want to rehire them at some point. Simply put, Mutual Force provides a great way to keep contact with your ex-employees.
